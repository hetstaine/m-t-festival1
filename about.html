About Out of Time Festival

Where music transcends hours and every beat takes you on a journey beyond time.

Our Story

Founded in 2021, Out of Time Festival celebrates music in all its forms, bringing together fans, artists, and unforgettable vibes. From electrifying rock to smooth pop and classic oldies, every stage offers a unique experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a festival where time feels suspended and music speaks louder than words. We aim to unite communities, ignite creativity, and make memories that resonate long after the last note fades.

Festival Highlights

  • 3 stages with over 10 international bands
  • Workshops and jam sessions for music lovers
  • Food trucks, art installations, and immersive experiences
  • Eco-friendly practices and sustainable festival design

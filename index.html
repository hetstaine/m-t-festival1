Festival Bands Lineup

The Neon Beats

Formed in 2015, The Neon Beats electrified the underground scene with pulsating synth-rock. Known for their immersive live shows.

Popular Songs:

  • City Lights
  • Electric Pulse
  • Midnight Groove

Blue Harmony

Blue Harmony emerged in 2010 blending soulful pop and smooth jazz. Their tunes are a mix of chill vibes and catchy hooks.

Popular Songs:

  • Ocean Breeze
  • Skyline Dreams
  • Moonlit Melody

Golden Echo

Golden Echo, established in 2005, keeps oldies alive with modern flair. Their concerts are a nostalgic trip for all generations.

Popular Songs:

  • Timeless Tune
  • Retro Vibes
  • Golden Days
